# Privacy Policy – WholeMart

_Last updated: 2025-12-10_

Welcome to **WholeMart**.  
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
We may collect the following personal data when you register or use the app:
- Name  
- Phone number  
- Location (if you choose to share it)  

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
We may collect:
- Device information (Android version, model)  
- App usage data  
- Crash logs  

### 1.3 Firebase Services
Our app uses **Firebase** for:
- Authentication  
- Firestore Database  
- Cloud Messaging  

Firebase may automatically collect certain data as described in Google’s Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use collected information to:
- Create and manage your account  
- Provide product listings and services  
- Improve app performance and user experience  
- Contact you for order updates or support  

---

## 3. Sharing Your Information
We **do not sell or trade** your personal data.  
Information may be shared only with:
- Firebase (for backend services)  
- Third-party service providers required for app functionality  

---

## 4. Data Security
We use modern security techniques to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

---

## 5. User Rights
You have the right to:
- Request deletion of your account  
- Request access to the information we have stored  
- Stop sharing location access at any time via device settings  

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy
Our app does **not target children under 13**.
